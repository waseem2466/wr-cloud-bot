# WR POS Return Policy
- Items can be returned within 3 days of purchase.
- Receipt must be present.
- Cash refunds are not given; store credit is issued instead.
- Electronics have a 7-day warranty for manufacturing defects only.

# WR POS Delivery Rules
- Delivery is available for orders over Rs. 1000.
- Delivery fees: Rs. 200 for Trincomalee, Rs. 400 for other districts.
- Delivery takes 2-3 working days.
- Cash on Delivery is NOT available; Bank Transfer required.

# WR POS Warranty
- Phone accessories: No warranty.
- Kitchen appliances: 1-year warranty.
- Stationery: No warranty.
- Cosmetics: No warranty, check expiry date at purchase.
